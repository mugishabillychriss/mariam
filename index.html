About Mariam Handwork

Mariam Handwork is a Rwandan bag manufacturing business dedicated to producing high-quality, handmade leather and artisan bags. Founded by Mariam, our mission is to promote the "Made in Rwanda" brand through stylish, durable products that tell a story.

We combine local materials with skilled Rwandan craftsmanship to create bags that are both beautiful and affordable. Every bag is carefully crafted by local artisans who take pride in their work, ensuring each piece is unique and of the highest quality.

Our vision is to make Made in Rwanda bags a recognized global brand while empowering local communities through sustainable employment.
